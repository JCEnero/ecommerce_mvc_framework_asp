## ?? Micro-interactions

### **Hover Effects:**
- Card lift (translateY -8px)
- Image zoom & rotate
- Button glow increase
- Price color change to gold
- Border color brightening

### **Button Animations:**
- Scale down on press (0.95)
- Lift on hover (-2px)
- Ripple effect (checkout button)
- Rotation (remove buttons)

### **Smooth Transitions:**
- All: `0.3s - 0.4s ease`
- Images: `0.4s - 0.6s cubic-bezier`
- Complex: `cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)`

- Grid: 2-3 columns
- Adjusted padding

### **Mobile (< 768px)**
- Sidebar: Full width, no sticky
- Grid: 2 columns (minmax 160px)
- Order summary: Fixed bottom
- Larger tap targets
- Reduced padding
