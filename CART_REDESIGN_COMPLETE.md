## ?? Design Details

### **Color Palette**
- Background: `#0D0D0D` (Deep Black)
- Card Background: `#1A1A1A` to `#141414` (gradient)
- Accent Gold: `#C9A961`
- Text White: `#FFFFFF`
- Text Gray: `#999`, `#666`
- Border: `#252525`

### **Typography**
- Titles: `Poppins` - 300/400/500/600 weight
- Body: `Inter` - 400/500 weight
- Labels: `Montserrat` - 500 weight uppercase

### **Spacing**
- Card padding: 30px (desktop), 20px (mobile)
- Gap between elements: 20-30px
- Border radius: 16px (cards), 50px (buttons)
